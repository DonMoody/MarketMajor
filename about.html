About Us

Market Major Hawaii is designed by UHH student, Donald Moody, as a way to uplit his community and bring the necessary tools and information needed for any individual to succeed in small business on Hawaii island.

Right now, the goal is to bring these tools to Hawaii island but, in the near future, we aim to bring these tools all across Hawaii and eventually the rest of the U.S.

Are you brave enough to take the risk and reap the reward? With our tools and your business decision, success with small business is right at your fingertips.
