Essential Business Resources

Click on any logo below to visit a helpful website for starting, planning, and managing your small business.

Hawaii DCCA

The Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs is the official resource for registering your business, licensing, and regulatory information in Hawaii. Hawaii did a great job of making resources on this website easy to find.

Small Business Administration (SBA)

A government agency that provides counseling, capital, and contracting expertise. It is dedicated to helping small businesses start, grow, and succeed across the nation.

Internal Revenue Service (IRS)

The source for tax information, forms, and publications necessary for managing your federal business tax obligations and avoiding tax penalties. Every business owner should get familiar with this website.

Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)

A principal federal agency that gives you insight into the labor market activity, working conditions, and price changes in the economy. Useful for choosing and understanding a business.

Squareup

A great website for tracking and processing card market sales. You also get a free Square terminal which save you money.
